❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lunora?

Lunora is the first Islamic app designed specifically for Muslim women. It includes the full Quran with translation, daily duas, a Ramadan tracker, gratitude journal, and a thoughtful Period Mode — all in a beautiful, private, and judgment-free space.

What is Period Mode?

Period Mode is a unique feature that gently adapts your spiritual experience during your menstrual cycle. It provides curated duas, dhikr, mood and symptom tracking, and comforting reminders — all while respecting Islamic guidelines. Your health data stays fully private on your device.

Is Lunora free?

Lunora is free to download and use. Core features like Quran reading, prayer times, duas, and Period Mode are available at no cost. We believe every Muslim woman deserves access to quality spiritual tools.

Which countries are supported?

Lunora is available in 200+ countries worldwide through the App Store. No matter where you are, you can access accurate prayer times, the full Quran, and all of Lunora's features. Google Play support is coming soon.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All personal data, including Period Mode health tracking, is stored locally on your device using encryption. We never collect, share, or sell your data. Lunora is designed with a privacy-first philosophy.
